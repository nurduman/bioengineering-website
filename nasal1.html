Hybrid CFD/PINN tool for treatment management of patients with nasal septum deviation

About

The purpose of the study is to develop a tool for treatment management of patients with NSD, based on CFD modeling technology combined with PINN programming. The tool includes the integration of CFD modeling data into PINN programming using active anterior rhinomanometry data. This tool will help identify airflow problems in a specific area of the nasal cavity and sinuses.
